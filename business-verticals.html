What We Do

Green Valley Nursery operates across multiple business verticals to provide comprehensive, end-to-end solutions for creating and maintaining clean, safe, and green environments.

Horticulture & Landscaping

Transforming outdoor spaces with expert garden development, lawn maintenance, plantation, and modern irrigation systems to enhance natural beauty.

Facility Management

Delivering integrated facility management solutions that ensure smooth operations, proper technical maintenance, and supervision for organizations.

Manpower Supply

Providing reliable skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled workforce to meet the operational, administrative, and maintenance needs of our clients.

Security Services

Offering disciplined and well-trained security personnel to ensure the safety and protection of government departments and commercial establishments.

Housekeeping Services

Maintaining pristine and hygienic environments using modern tools, effective cleaning agents, and trained staff for spotlessly clean premises.

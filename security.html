Security Services

Protecting What Matters Most to You

Dependable Manned Guarding & Security

Safety and security are the paramount concerns for any establishment. At Green Valley Nursery, we provide highly trained, alert, and professional security personnel to safeguard your assets, employees, and premises.

We serve a wide spectrum of clients including corporate hubs, industrial sites, retail environments, and residential societies. Our guards undergo rigorous background checks and training in access control, emergency response, and public relations to ensure a safe, yet welcoming environment.

  • Manned Guarding: 24/7 presence of trained security guards and supervisors.
  • Access Control: Strict monitoring of visitors, material movement, and vehicle logs.
  • Emergency Response: Personnel trained in fire safety, evacuation, and first aid.
  • Patrolling & Surveillance: Regular night patrols and coordination with CCTV monitoring.
